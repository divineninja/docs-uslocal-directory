# Customer Walkthrough Guide

This guide provides a step-by-step walkthrough of the customer workflow for completing the full registration and onboarding process, including service agreements, ID verification, and payment authorization. The walkthrough is based on the screenshots in the `Customer` directory.

---

## Step 1: Introduction Email
![Introduction Email](./Customer/1.%20Introduction%20Email.png)

**Context:** The customer receives an introduction email initiating the registration process and providing initial instructions.

**Key Elements:**
- Welcome message and process overview
- Links or buttons to start the registration
- Contact information for support

**Instructions:**
1. Open the introduction email from the service provider.
2. Review the welcome message and understand the registration process.
3. Click the provided link or button to begin the onboarding.

![Service Agreement Page](./Customer/2.%20Service%20Agreement%20page%20-%20customer%20will%20sign%20to%20proceed.png)

## Workflow Summary

This customer workflow demonstrates the complete onboarding and registration process:

1. **Introduction** - Receive initial email and start process
2. **Agreements** - Review and sign service agreements and authorizations
3. **Verification** - Confirm signup and upload ID for verification
4. **Welcome** - Receive confirmation and access materials

## Notes

- The workflow includes multiple confirmation and thank you pages for user reassurance
- ID verification is a key step for security and compliance
- PDF documents are provided for record-keeping and reference

## For Developers

When implementing this workflow:
- Ensure secure document upload and storage
- Implement electronic signature capabilities
- Provide clear progress indicators throughout the process
- Include proper validation for all user inputs
- Support multiple document formats and sizes
- Maintain audit trails for all agreements and uploads
